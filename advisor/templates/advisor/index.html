About Project

1. What is the need?

Hyderabad faces severe air pollution, which has a significant impact on the health and well-being of its residents. Our project aims to address this problem by utilizing plants to reduce harmful pollutants naturally.

2. Why This Project is Better?

• No electricity needed
• No ozone emission
• Cost-effective and eco-friendly
• Direct improvement of air quality

3. How often can we use this project?

• The system can be accessed at any time for continuous monitoring of air quality.
• Real-time plant suggestions are available based on current pollution levels.

4. Input:

Monitored pollutants like CO, NO₂, SO₂, PM2.5, and PM10, collected from sensors placed in the vicinity of the user's location.

5. Output:

Our system recommends the best plants suitable for improving air quality by absorbing harmful pollutants. The recommendations are tailored to the specific pollutant levels detected.

6. Impact of Pollution:

The air pollution in urban areas like Hyderabad leads to severe health issues, including respiratory diseases, lung cancer, and cardiovascular diseases. Our plant recommendations aim to mitigate these effects by utilizing the natural air-purifying abilities of specific plants.
