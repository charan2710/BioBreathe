Areca Palm

Best for NO₂ and SO₂. Requires watering every 2-3 days.

Maintenance: Water every 2-3 days, keep in indirect sunlight.

Best for NO₂. Requires moderate sunlight and watering every 3 days.

Snake Plant

Best for CO, PM2.5, PM10. Water once a week.

Maintenance: Water once a week, low light tolerant.

Maintenance: Indirect sunlight, water every 2 days.
